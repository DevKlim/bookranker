Here will list all unlockable content that persists for all levels (acc-wide).

Types of unlockables:
- recipes
- mods
    - unlocked by acheivements or conditions
    - mods are either dropped by enemies, produced with special buildings,
        in random loot chests, or as a random reward after each wave
    - building mods: power up one building
        - simple effects include increasing damage
        - complex effects include using conditions and reworks like:
            Undervolted: if not enough energy is supplied to the building,
            the building will be afflicted with frost and still power
            at a fraction dependent on the available power from the source.
    - wire mods: power up buildings connected to the segment of wires
    - core mods: affects the entire level
- allies
    - unlocked by special conditions
    - allies appear in a level by random event if valid for that level or
        upon starting level

## Mods

### Overheat
- Type: Building
- Unlock: Base(no need to unlock, started off unlocked).
- Get: Chance of dropping by killing enemy with Igni (1/50 chance).
- Effect: Increases Damage by 2%, but building takes 1 HP damage per second. Requires active repair or healing

### Battery
- Type: Building
- Unlock: Base(no need to unlock, started off unlocked).
- Get: Chance of dropping by killing enemy with Volt (1/50 chance).
- Effect: Stores up to 500 excess Power. Releases it when grid demand > generation.

### Shield
- Type: Building
- Unlock: Base(no need to unlock, started off unlocked).
- Get: Chance of dropping by killing enemy with Magne (1/50 chance).
- Effect: Regenerative shield of 10 HP

### Coolant
- Type: Building
- Unlock: Base(no need to unlock, started off unlocked).
- Get: Chance of dropping by killing enemy with Aqua (1/50 chance).
- Effect: Passive healing of 1HP/sec. Reduces Fire Rate by 2%.

### Harden
- Type: Building
- Unlock: Base(no need to unlock, started off unlocked).
- Get: Chance of dropping by killing enemy with Tera (1/50 chance).
- Effect: Multiplies Max HP by 1.2x. The building can no longer be rotated or moved (Must be "bombed" to remove).

### Vent
- Type: Building
- Unlock: Base(no need to unlock, started off unlocked).
- Get: Chance of dropping by killing enemy with Aero (1/50 chance).
- Effect: Nearby buildings will obtain 1% of this building's stats

### Catalyst
- Type: Building
- Unlock: Base(no need to unlock, started off unlocked).
- Get: Chance of dropping by killing enemy with Chem (1/50 chance).
- Effect: Increase Duration stat of Building by 5%

### Prism
- Type: Building
- Unlock: Base(no need to unlock, started off unlocked).
- Get: Chance of dropping by killing enemy with Lux (1/100 chance).
- Effect: Turns Building to scale with Magic Attack.
