Game has a Y2K theming with Frutiger Aero and Y2K Futurism driving its narrative.

Each level revolves around completing a specfic task despite its roguelike nature. The first level focuses on just defeating the final boss. The other levels focus around resolving a character's (allies) task.

The player awakens with no memories, next to a core that encases a young lady. The player, throughout the game, will come to realize that its his girlfriend from the real world. The player is given one task by the encased girl, to retrieve the lost souls and guide them back to the past.

In the real world, the young lady activated a time travel spell to revive her lost classmates and friends as a tragic accident occured. Now in this afterlife world, she is going against the martial law of the world and the robotic world is against her. The player must go and seek each ally/person and take them back to the real world and the past.

Each level has a theme. The gameplay loop typically revolves around defending the core as normal, building a defense and building a factory to sustain and stay in the world. There are two objectives: help the ally move on and defeat the final boss.
