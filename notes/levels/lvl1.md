# Level 1: Back to Basics

**Theme:** Classroom & Stationery Factory
**Background:** Chalkboard (DON'T CHANGE)
**Terrain:** Marble tiles (Floor uses the Marble block rather than stone)

## Core Mechanic: Number Cruncher
- A random integer from `1` to `9` for that run/seed is displayed.
- **Synergy:** If at any time a source of damage exactly equals that generated number, that damage is instantly **doubled**. Weapons like the *Picasso* or specific highly-tuned mod chip setups allow players to snipe this multiplier consistently. Damage must be perfect and decimals do not count.

## Starting Conditions
- **Currency:** 4 Byts
- **Inventory:** 1x Hand Drill, 10x Marble
- **Grid Setup:** 5 Lanes, Default Difficulty

## Gameplay Flow
1. **Initial Shop:** Upon starting, the player will immediately load into a shop prompt.
2. **Purchasing Mods:** Players can exchange Byts (currency) for Building Mods (Disks) and Core Mods (HDDs).
3. **Core Mods:** The very first shop guarantees three HDDs (Core Mods) that will permanently modify the run. 
4. **Subsequent Shops:** After the initial phase, shops will prompt after each wave and sell any unlocked mods (up to 3) from the global pool.

## Buildings
- **Chalkboard (Tier 1 Crafter):** The fundamental crafting hub. *(Cost: 15x Ignichalk, 15x Aquachalk, 15x Aerochalk, 5x Graphite, 5x Marble)*
- **Drill:** Extracts raw materials from the ground deposits. *(Cost: 10x Marble, 10x Graphite, 5x Aerochalk)*
- **Binder:** Acts as a high-capacity storage unit. *(Cost: 5x Cardboard)*
- **Printer:** Generates Paper over time, requires energy. *(Cost: 15x Graphite, 10x Ink, 10x Ignichalk, 10x Aerochalk, 10x Aquachalk)*
- **Foldgami:** The core ammunition assembler. Takes Paper + any Elemental Chalk (or Ink) which is optional + a Stamp(if no stamp, it is crumpled), and folds them into an infused Origami Weapon. *(Cost: 10x Graphite, 2x Ink)*
- **Box Fan:** Placed behind a Foldgami. Blows the folded creations towards enemies, making them move like projectiles. Applies Aero to all enemies within a range of 3 in front of it. *(Cost: 10x Graphite, 6x Cardboard, 4x Aerochalk)*
- **Slipslide:** A transport building. Places a slide where exported items glide along the wet ground until hitting another building or the stream ends. Takes items from the building behind it. *(Cost: 5x Graphite, 2x Ignichalk, 3x Aquachalk)*
- **Slipstream:** A placeable water puddle stream. Essential for sea-based origami travel (fast speed). *(Cost: 2x Aquachalk)*
- **Tarstream:** A placeable ink puddle stream. Used for sea-based origami (slow speed, multi-hit). **Applies Slime to enemies.** *(Cost: 2x Ink, 2x Aquachalk)*

## Deposits & Drilled Materials
*Ores are found as deposits in the ground and must be extracted using a Drill or Hand Drill.*
- **Deposits:** Marble Deposit, Graphite Deposit, Ink Deposit, Ignichalk Deposit, Aquachalk Deposit, Aerochalk Deposit, Luxchalk Deposit.
- **Yields:** Marble, Graphite, Ink, Ignichalk, Aquachalk, Aerochalk, Luxchalk.

## Crafted Materials & Stamps
- **Cardboard:** *(Cost: 5x Paper, 1x Tarstream, 1x Aerochalk)*
- **Paper:** Generated by Printer.
- **Whiteboard:** Grants direct access to Chalkboard crafting from anywhere in your inventory. *(Cost: 5x Ink, 4x Graphite)*
- **Stamps:** Inserted into the Foldgami to determine the output geometry.
  - **Plane Stamp:** *(Cost: 1x Tarstream, 1x Paper, 1x Aerochalk)*
  - **Crane Stamp / Swan Stamp / Lotus Stamp / Shuriken Stamp:** *(RNG Obtain)*

## Folds (Origami Weapons)
*Folds are the outputs of the Foldgami. Their elemental damage is dictated by the Chalk infused during crafting (e.g., Ignichalk makes it deal Igni damage, Ink makes it apply Slime).*
- **Air-Borne (Planefold, Shurikenfold, Cranefold):** Travel by air and deal direct damage. Cranefolds inherently pierce enemies.
- **Ground-Borne (Crumpledfold):** Roll on the ground and disappear upon colliding with any enemy.
  - *Slipstream:* Travels faster.
  - *Tarstream (Ink):* Travels slower and multi-hit.
- **Sea-Borne (Swanfold, Lotusfold):** Must travel via **Slipstream** or **Tarstream**. If they leave the stream, they vanish.
  - *Slipstream:* Travels fast, deals 1 instance of damage. Swanfold coasts to the end.
  - *Tarstream (Ink):* Travels slow, multi-hit. Lotusfold explodes upon impact.

## Random Events

### Heated Up!
- All enemies will have a permanent Igni inflicted but moves 1.5x faster for the rest of the wave.
- Chance: 2%
- When: Upon wave start.

  - Wave 1: 2m | Wave 2: 4m | Wave 3: 2m | Wave 4: 2m | Wave 5: 2m | Wave 6: 4m | Wave 7: 4m

## Special / Elite Units

### Ecaker 
*   **Description:** A walking eraser with his bottom so big, it touches the ground!
*   **Mechanic:** Slow speed, burst damage, tanky.
*   **Special (Wipe Clean):** As it walks over **Slipstreams** or **Tarstreams**, it completely erases them from the grid, ruining sea-borne Origami supply lines. 

## Bosses

### Enormous Ecaker (Wave 3 Boss)
*   **Description:** A big ecaker that ground slams buildings and one shots them. Ignores allies. After slamming, bounces back 2 tiles and to a new lane.
*   **Mechanic:** Periodically slashes the grid, creating temporary walls of red ink that block Origami projectiles from passing through. 

### Inkage (Level Boss)
*   **Description:** A ink-slathered mage who will inflict his inkmagic on you.
*   **Mechanic:** Does not approach the core but moves side to side and teleports sometimes. He remains atleast 5 tiles away from the nearest building or at the end of the lane. 
* **Attacks:**
- Periodically sends inked paper planes down the lane with 3 HP. Will do large damage upon contact.
- Teleports and fires an ink bullet down the lane it teleports to.

## Weapons/Tools
### Picasso
- Type: Weapon
- Unlock: RNG Obtain.
- Effect: Consumes a variable amount of Ink (`N`, between 1-9) from the ally's inventory to deal exactly `N` damage. The charge time to unleash damage scales by 0.5 seconds per `N` added (base 1 second). Paints the number on the enemy and applies the **Slime** reaction. Cooldown: 2s.
- Synergy: Perfect for sniping the Level 1 "Number Cruncher" mechanic.

### Yardstick
- Type: Weapon
- Unlock: Crafting (12x Ink, 12x Graphite, 2x Ignichalk, 1x Aerochalk).
- Effect: Deals 3 damage per hit. Has 52 uses. Cooldown: 1s.

### Dull Pencil
- Type: Weapon
- Unlock: Crafting (16x Graphite, 2x Ignichalk) or degrading a Sharpened Pencil.
- Effect: Deals 1 damage every 2 seconds.

### Sharp Pencil
- Type: Weapon
- Unlock: Crafting (8x Graphite, 1x Sharpened Pencil).
- Effect: Deals 1 damage every 1 seconds. Degrades after 64 uses.


### Hand Drill
- Type: Tool
- Unlock: Base (Started off unlocked).
- Crafting: 5x Marble.
- Effect: Basic manual extraction tool for ores/materials.
