Here will list all unlockable content that persists for all levels (acc-wide).

Types of unlockables:
- recipes
- mods
    - unlocked by acheivements or conditions
    - mods are either dropped by enemies, produced with special buildings,
        in random loot chests, or as a random reward after each wave
    - building mods(flash drive): power up one building
        - simple effects include increasing damage
        - complex effects include using conditions and reworks like:
            Undervolted: if not enough energy is supplied to the building,
            the building will be afflicted with frost and still power
            at a fraction dependent on the available power from the source.
    - wire mods(amps): power up buildings connected to the segment of wires
    - core mods(sd cards): affects the entire level
- allies
    - unlocked by special conditions
    - allies appear in a level by random event if valid for that level or
        upon starting level
